Founded in 2007, The Design Office is a place that supports independent practioners. Members manage their own practice, while inevitably partnering for larger commissions, grants, and other self-initiated projects.

The fellowship is meant for recent graduates (Masters or Bachelors) interested in pursuing independent practice.

Fellows work independently.

Paid commissions or projects may arise during the fellowship, but applicants are expected to have their own projects or project ideas as a basis for the fellowship.
